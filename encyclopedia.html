Kenya

Location

Kenya is a country located in east Africa and has a coast on the Indian Ocean. It is located on the equator, so it has a tropical climate with only two seasons: rain and dry season.

History

Before becoming a country, Kenya was a British colony. Through the efforts of the Mau Mau rebellion, Kenya gained independence from Great Britain in 1963 and became a republic in 1964.

Nature

Kenya's climate allows for the growth of many different fruits and vegetables. The most abundant plants being grown in Kenya are tea and coffee; in fact, Kenya is the largest exporter of tea in all of Africa. Kenya is also home to lots of wildlife, inlcuding Africa's Big Five. Many tourists visit Kenya to go on safaris and watch these animals in the wild.
